Overview

Truck Lagbe aims to revamp the existing Business Portal which will help in handling our Brokerage client’s operational process and it will also help Business Team to retain and onboard new clients in our trucking ecosystem while revamping it we should consider SAAS as we will be building it next.

Problem Statement

Despite the growing demand for efficient and cost-effective transportation solutions in the trucking industry, many businesses in Bangladesh struggle to find a suitable software platform that meets their specific needs. Existing solutions are often expensive, difficult to use, and lack the necessary features to effectively manage and track their trucking operations. As a result, many businesses continue to rely on manual processes and spreadsheets, resulting in inefficiencies, errors, and increased costs. Our trucking platform aims to fill this gap in the market by providing a cost-effective, user-friendly, and feature-rich software solution that addresses the specific needs of businesses in the Bangladeshi trucking industry.

Goal
