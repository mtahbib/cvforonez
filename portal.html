Problem Statement

Despite the growing demand for efficient and cost-effective transportation solutions in the trucking industry, many businesses in Bangladesh struggle to find a suitable software platform that meets their specific needs. Existing solutions are often expensive, difficult to use, and lack the necessary features to effectively manage and track their trucking operations. As a result, many businesses continue to rely on manual processes and spreadsheets, resulting in inefficiencies, errors, and increased costs. Our trucking platform aims to fill this gap in the market by providing a cost-effective, user-friendly, and feature-rich software solution that addresses the specific needs of businesses in the Bangladeshi trucking industry.

User Stories

Feature User Story Acceptance Criteria
Sign Up for Business Portal As a corporate client, I want to be able to sign up for the Business Portal so that I can efficiently run my trucking operation.
  1. The corporate client can access the sign-up page.
  2. The sign-up form includes fields for relevant information (e.g., company name, contact information).
  3. The client can submit the sign-up form.
  4. Upon successful submission, the client receives a confirmation email.
Sign In to Business Portal As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to sign in so that I can access the available features of the Business Portal.
  1. The Business Portal has a sign-in page accessible to registered users.
  2. Users can enter their credentials (e.g., email and password) to sign in.
  3. Valid credentials grant access to the Business Portal dashboard.
Real-time Dashboard As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to have a real-time view of my activity so that I can analyze and track my trucking operation.
  1. The dashboard provides a real-time view of the corporate client's trucking operation activity.
  2. Data on the dashboard updates dynamically, reflecting the latest information.
View Real-time Analytics As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to view real-time data, generate reports in CSV format, and filter the data using available options so that I can analyze and track my trucking operation.
  1. Users can access an analytics section in the Business Portal.
  2. Real-time data is displayed in a user-friendly format (e.g., graphs, charts).
  3. Users can generate reports in CSV format from the analytics section.
  4. Filtering options are available to refine the displayed data (e.g., date range, specific metrics).
Trip Summary As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to check the summary of my trips so that I can track the records to increase my operational efficiency.
  1. Users can navigate to a "Trip Summary" section within the Business Portal.
  2. The "Trip Summary" section displays a summarized list of all trips, including relevant details (e.g., date, destination).
  3. Users can efficiently track and analyze trip records from this section.
Team Access Control As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to create, edit, and delete groups of users and assign them specific access rights to certain functions of the Business Portal so that I can manage user access and ensure that only authorized users can perform certain actions.
  1. Users with appropriate permissions can access the "Team Access Control" section.
  2. The section allows users to create groups of users and assign specific access rights to functions of the Business Portal.
  3. Users can edit existing groups and update access rights as needed.
  4. Users can delete groups that are no longer required.
Change Password As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to change my password so that I can secure my own account.
  1. Users can access their profile settings within the Business Portal.
  2. A "Change Password" option is available in the profile settings.
  3. Users can enter their current password and a new password for security purposes.
  4. Password change is successful, and the new password is immediately active for the user's account.
Fleet Management As a Business Portal user, I want to be able to provide suggestions for improving our fleet management system so that I can contribute to the enhancement of our operations.
  1. The Business Portal includes a user-friendly input section where users can enter suggestions.
  2. The input section enforces a word limit of 1000 characters for user suggestions.
  3. Upon clicking "Submit" or "Interested," the user's feedback is logged in a dedicated Google Sheet for further review.
Vendor Management As a Business Portal user, I want to have the ability to share suggestions for optimizing our vendor management process, contributing to stronger client relationships.
  1. The Business Portal provides a user-friendly input section for users to submit suggestions.
  2. The input section enforces a word limit of 1000 characters for user suggestions.
  3. When users click "Submit" or "Interested," their feedback is recorded in a dedicated Google Sheet for review.
My Trips As a Business Portal user, I want a centralized platform to manage and track my business trips conveniently, improving trip planning and organization.
  1. Create a dedicated section in the Business Portal for users to manage all business trips, including upcoming and historical trips.
  2. Display a visually appealing summary of upcoming and past trips with key details such as trip names, dates, and destinations.
  3. Make each trip entry clickable, leading to a detailed view of that specific trip.
  4. Organize trips into "Current Trips" (Bid On Going, In Review) and "Trip History" (Finished, Canceled) categories based on their status.
